Utilizing state-of-the-art equipment, our board-certified specialists and licensed support staff are dedicated to providing the highest standards of care in the field of sleep and neurodiagnostic medicine to improve the quality of life for our patients.

Samuel Sims, RPSGT, is a registered polysomnographic technologist who was the former chief polysomnographic technologist for Bellaire General Hospital and the former chief polysomnographic consultant for St. Joseph Hospital.

Services for the diagnosis and treatment of sleep disorders:
  • Diagnostic PSG (sleep study)
  • Positive Airway Pressure (Continuous and Bi-level) titrations
  • Split-night studies
  • Assessment of daytime sleepiness (Multiple Sleep Latency test and Maintenance of Wakefulness test)
  • Consultations with Sleep Medicine Specialists
  • Average volume assisted pressure unit or device
  • BiLevel auto servo ventilation

Sugar Land Sleep, LLC./Sims Management Inc. offers both in-lab and at-home portable testing to ensure you receive the care that best suits your condition. We provide neurodiagnostic management from your preliminary diagnosis to the initiation of treatment.
